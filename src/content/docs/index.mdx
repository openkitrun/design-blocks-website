# Join the Community

Be a part of our growing Design Blocks community! Whether you're looking for support, want to contribute, or simply connect with fellow developers, we welcome you.

## Get Involved

- **GitHub Repository:** Check out our [GitHub](https://github.com/openkitrun/design-blocks) to contribute, report issues, or request new features.
- **Community Forum:** Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/yCvjTtT2hD) to discuss ideas, share projects, and get help.
- **Social Media:** Follow us on [X](https://twitter.com/designblocksdev) for the latest updates, tips, and more.

## Support

Need help or have questions? Our community is here to assist. Reach out on our forum or directly on GitHub.

Together, let's build something amazing!
